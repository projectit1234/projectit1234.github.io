Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a coffee drink that originated in Italy, traditionally prepared with espresso, hot milk, and steamed milk foam. It is typically smaller in volume than a latte, and has a strong, bold flavor. Cappuccino is often garnished with cinnamon or cocoa powder.

Specification Composition Preparation
Quantity One cup
Ingredients
  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1/3 cup of steamed milk
  • 1/3 cup of milk foam
  • Cinnamon or cocoa powder for garnish
Preparation
  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Steam milk until it reaches a creamy texture.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso in a cup.
  4. Add the milk foam on top.
  5. Sprinkle with cinnamon or cocoa powder.
