Syrian Shawarma with Meat

Syrian Shawarma with meat is a traditional Syrian dish consisting of thinly sliced grilled meat with special spices such as cumin, coriander, and Eastern spices. It is placed in Arabic bread with mixed vegetables and tahini sauce. Syrian Shawarma with meat is usually served as a light meal or a quick meal.

Watch how to prepare

Here's a simple recipe for preparing Syrian-style shawarma:

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of minced beef (you can use chicken if you prefer)
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of shawarma spices
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Arabic bread for serving
  • Vegetables for serving (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, green pepper)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the oil and then add the chopped onion and garlic. Stir until they become golden brown.
  2. Add the minced beef to the skillet and stir well with the onion and garlic until cooked and the color turns brown.
  3. Add the shawarma spices, salt, and black pepper, and continue cooking while stirring for an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Let the meat cool slightly, then place it in Arabic bread and add the vegetables such as lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and green pepper sliced into strips.
  5. Now you can wrap the bread and serve the delicious Syrian shawarma!

You can also add tahini or yogurt to the shawarma sauce according to your taste, and you can serve it with French fries or assorted salads. Enjoy your meal!
