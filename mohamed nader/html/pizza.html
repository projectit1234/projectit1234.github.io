Preparation steps!

  1. Preheat the oven to 260 degrees Celsius.
  2. Take a small bowl and add olive oil and garlic.
  3. Stir together, then spread evenly over the surface and edges of the dough.
  4. Spread about two-thirds of a cup of pizza sauce over the dough.
  5. Spread the cheese evenly, then the pepperoni slices.
  6. Serve the pepperoni pizza hot.
