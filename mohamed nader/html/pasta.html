Preparation steps!

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. Place the Flora pasta tray on the rack in the middle of the oven.
  3. Bake it for 40-45 minutes.
  4. Take it out and let it cool completely.
  5. Cut the pasta flora into several equal pieces.
  6. Serve Flora pasta cold or warm as desired.
