cooking Basbousa

compnents and Ingredients

compnents Ingredients (gram)
Fine semolina 300
sugar 150
Grated coconut 100
Melted butter 150
Yogurt 250
Baking powder 10
Vanilla 2
sherbet 650

method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. In a bowl, mix semolina, sugar, coconut, baking powder, and vanilla.
  3. Add melted butter and yogurt to the mixture.
  4. Mix the ingredients well until you get a homogeneous consistency.
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased tray.
  6. Cut the basbousa into squares and place a piece of almond or walnut on each square.
  7. Bake the basbousa in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden.
  8. After the basbousa has finished baking, pour the syrup over it while it is hot.
  9. Allow the basbousa to absorb the syrup, then serve it.
