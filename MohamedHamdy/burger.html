Burger



Ingredients
1/4 kg Beef
1/2 minced Onion
Cheese
1 Egg
28 grams Dry onion powder
1 table spoon Garlic powder
1 teaspoon Soy Sauce
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon crushed Rosemary
1 clove of minced garlic
salt & pepper

Instructions
Step 1 combine beef, onion, cheese, egg, dry onion powder, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
Step 2 cook patties on a preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side.
