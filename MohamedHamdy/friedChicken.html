Fried Chicken


    Ingredients

  • 1 chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 litres vegetable oil for frying

    Instructions

  1. Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin them if you prefer
  2. Put the flour in a large plastic bag (let the amount of chicken you are cooking dictate the amount of flour you use). Season the flour with paprika, salt and pepper
  3. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk then, a few at a time, put them in the bag with the flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well

    4. buttermilk in home

  4. Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper. LET SIT UNTIL THE FLOUR IS OF A PASTE-LIKE CONSISTENCY. THIS IS CRUCIAL!
  5. Fry the chicken till brown on both sides
  6. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet let cook for 30 minutes (the chicken will be cooked through but not crispy). Remove cover, raise heat again, and continue to fry until crispy
